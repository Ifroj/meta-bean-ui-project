Elite Training Programs

Transforming civilians into warriors through rigorous and specialized training programs designed to build physical strength, mental resilience, and tactical expertise.

Training Categories

BASIC 26 Weeks

Basic Military Training

Foundation training for all recruits covering weapon handling, drill, physical fitness, and military discipline.

  • Weapons training (INSAS, AK-47)
  • Field craft and battle tactics
  • Map reading and navigation
  • First aid and survival skills
All Recruits Details
ELITE 90 Weeks

Para Special Forces

Elite counter-terrorism and special operations training for selected personnel.

  • HALO/HAHO parachute training
  • Hostage rescue operations
  • Counter-insurgency warfare
  • Underwater operations
Volunteers Only Details
TECHNICAL 72 Weeks

Army Aviation Corps

Comprehensive pilot training for rotary-wing aircraft operations.

  • Fixed and rotary wing training
  • Night flying certification
  • Combat search and rescue
  • Medical evacuation operations

Training Requirements & Eligibility

Physical Standards

Height & Weight

  • • Men: Minimum 157.5 cm (relaxable for某些 regions)
  • • Women: Minimum 152 cm
  • • Weight proportionate to height and age

Physical Tests

  • • 1.6 km run: 5 minutes 30 seconds (Men)
  • • 800m run: 4 minutes (Women)
  • • Pull-ups: 6 (Men) / Flexed arm hang: 20 seconds (Women)
  • • 9 feet ditch and zig-zag balance

Medical Standards

Vision Requirements

  • • Distant vision: 6/6 in better eye, 6/9 in worse eye
  • • Near vision: N6 in better eye, N9 in worse eye
  • • Myopia not to exceed -3.5D
  • • No color blindness or night blindness

General Health

  • • Free from any disease or disability
  • • No history of mental illness or epilepsy
  • • Hearing: Normal hearing threshold
  • • No hernia, piles, or hydrocele
