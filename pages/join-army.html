Agniveer Scheme

Tour of Duty: Agniveer

Transformative 4-year service scheme for youth to serve as Agniveers. Opportunity to learn, earn, and contribute to national security.

  • 4-year service period
  • Attractive monthly package
  • Seva Nidhi package after completion
  • 25% retention for regular cadre
